About Us

Welcome to our company! At SecureBand, we are dedicated to creating innovative wearable safety devices for kids. Since its founding in January of 2023, our mission has been to provide peace of mind for parents in protecting their kids from tragic events by offering real-time tracking, emergency alerts, and geofencing capabilities using our state-of-the-art wristband technology.


With our commitment to technological innovation, SecureBand continuously updates its devices to incorporate the latest safety features, ensuring that parents have access to the most reliable and effective tools to keep their kids safe. We prioritize the safety and security of children, enabling parents to embrace a worry-free lifestyle while their little ones enjoy their everyday adventures.
